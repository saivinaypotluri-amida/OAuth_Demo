════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
    📝 HOW TO GENERATE SUMMARIES + SAVE TO GDRIVE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

EASIEST METHOD: WEB PORTAL
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to: http://localhost:3000/messages

2. Click green "Generate Summary" button (top-right)

3. Enter:
   - Title: "My Summary" (optional)
   - Content: Paste your long text (min 50 chars)

4. Click "Generate & Save to Drive"

5. Wait 3 seconds

6. Done! ✅
   - Summary shows on screen
   - Saved to Google Drive
   - Click link to open in Drive

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IN SLACK:
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Type in any channel:

  summarize: [paste your long text here]

Bot responds with summary + Google Drive link! 🎉

Or use @mention:

  @YourBot summarize: [your text]

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ALTERNATIVE SLACK KEYWORDS:
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  summary: [text]
  tldr: [text]
  tl;dr: [text]
  summarise: [text]

All work the same way!
